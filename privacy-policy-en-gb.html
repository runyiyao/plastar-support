Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 April 2026

Overview

Plastar ("the App") is developed by Runyi Yao. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. The App operates entirely offline with no internet connectivity required.

Camera and Photo Library

The App requires access to:

  • Camera: To capture photos for your film rolls.
  • Photo Library (write-only): To save developed photos to your device's photo library.

Photos and film roll data are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded to any server.

Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Film roll information (film stock, frame count, dates)
  • Captured photos (original and filtered versions)
  • App preferences (e.g. onboarding completion)

This data remains exclusively on your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Notifications

The App may request permission to send local notifications to remind you when your film roll has finished developing. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device — no push notification servers are involved.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

Tracking

The App does not track you in any way. No advertising identifiers, analytics tools, or behavioural tracking are used.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at yao.runyi33@outlook.com.
